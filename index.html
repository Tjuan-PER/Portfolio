👨🏽 ABOUT ME

I'm a recent graduate of the Master's of Data Science in Computational Linguistics at the University of British Columbia, Vancouver. Formerly a teacher for 4th grade, I have transitioned from wrangling 10-year-olds to wrangling data. Through this rigorous program I've been able to form foundational knowledge in statistics (inference and prediction), machine learning, and data engineering. At the same time, I continued to up-skill my understanding of linguistics and programming. This has allowed me to build projects centered around creating datasets and predictive models; performing sentiment analysis; conducting information retrieval and extraction; developing chatbots with natural language understanding and agentic workflow; training and fine-tuning models — just to name a few.
